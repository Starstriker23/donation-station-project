Welcome to The Donation Station, a web application created to support students by providing affordable access to gently used school supplies. This platform connects the campus community by giving donated items a second life, ensuring that students can find what they need without the burden of high costs.

From everyday essentials like binders and backpacks to clothing, sweaters, and even electronics, our directory makes it easy to browse and discover items that fit your academic and personal needs.

By offering supplies at prices far below market value, The Donation Station not only helps students save money but also promotes sustainability through reuse and recycling. Every item donated contributes to reducing waste and building a culture of sharing within the college.
